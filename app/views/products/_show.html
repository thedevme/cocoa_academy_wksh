Workshop Features

<%= image_tag('feature1.png', alt: 'Siri') %>

Siri

You will learn how to use "Hey, Siri" to request and send money. SiriKit was introduced in iOS 10 and we will the basics of what types of apps can get SiriKit.

<%= image_tag('feature2.png', alt: 'TouchID') %>

TouchID

We will use TouchID for security. In this app, we will create a log in for users to access with either an email or they can use their fingerprint to log into their account.

<%= image_tag('feature3.png', alt: 'Contacts') %>

Contacts

We will see how you can import a users contacts into the app, in order to send and request money to friends and family.

<%= image_tag('feature4.png', alt: 'Swift 3') %>

Swift 3

You will learn the lastest in Swift 3 for this workshop.

<%= image_tag('feature5.png', alt: 'Storyboard') %>

Storyboard

We will work with Storyboard in order to create all of our UI elements. Storyboard makes it easy for us to create apps that support multiple device sizes.
